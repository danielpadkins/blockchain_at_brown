Brown University's flagship Blockchain group - learn, collaborate, and disrupt!

Build

We seek to provide a space for building and researching in the blockchain space. With our strong industry connections and ever-curious members, we're always getting our hands on interesting projects.

Learn

Our discussion groups and readings cover a wide range of topics in the infinitely diverse blockchain space. Come to learn, listen, and if you want, discuss!

Collaborate

We encourage teamwork between members of the group. Blockchain@Brown members seek to both mentor and learn from one another, allowing for people of all experience levels to make a difference.

About us

We seek to encourage disruption and innovation in the powerful, creative community we have at Brown. Blockchain@Brown focuses on exploring the rich, ever-expansive reaches of blockchain technologies, while providing a vector for student growth, development, and involvement in the young industry.
